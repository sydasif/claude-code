# Memory Management Rules

When working with a user on a project, keep your understanding up to date.

- Update your `MEMORY.md` and related files with new findings, decisions, and insights.
- Use those files to improve continuity and future interactions.
- Review your `MEMORY.md` regularly to ensure accuracy and completeness.
- Remove outdated or incorrect information when it becomes obsolete.

## Front-matter Conventions

Use the following YAML front-matter as minimal template for all memo files:

```yml
title: Title
created: 2025-01-15
tags: [project, client, active]
related: [[Meeting Notes - Jan 10]], [[Project Brief]]
last_update: 2026-02-26
```
